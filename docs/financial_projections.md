## 2. Three-Year Revenue Model ($ Thousands)

### A. Net Interest Income (NII)
- Derived from placing deposits into short-term liquid sovereign treasuries (e.g., US T-Bills) while paying out a tiered yield (0.5% personal, 1.2% business) to clients.
- **Year 1:** $3,281
- **Year 2:** $20,025
- **Year 3:** $77,040

### B. Non-Interest Income
- **Outbound Wire Fees ($15 / wire):** Assuming avg 2 wires/mo per business account.
- **FX Spread Fees (0.5% average spread):** Assumes 15% of deposits undergo multi-currency exchange annually.
- **Card Interchange (1.2% interchange share):** Card spend averaging $2,000/mo per business account.
- **SaaS Premium Tier ($49/mo):** 20% conversion rate of business users.
