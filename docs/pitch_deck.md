## Slide 2: The Problem
Traditional offshore banking is broken.
- **Extreme Friction:** Months of onboarding time, physical paperwork, and archaic user interfaces.
- **Geographic Fragmentation:** Poor multi-currency capabilities. Businesses have to maintain separate bank relationships in different countries to handle USD, EUR, and GBP.
- **Inflexible Reconciliations:** High-net-worth entities and international businesses struggle to align records of historical cash flows with contract execution dates, causing tax and audit headaches.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
Meridian Trust Bank: The Sophisticated, High-Speed Offshore Fintech.
- **Self-Service Digital Onboarding:** Modern automated compliance lets clients register a personal or business account and get instant credentials.
- **Unified Multi-Currency Ledger:** Instantly provision Checking, Savings, and Money Market accounts in USD, EUR, and GBP.
- **Flexible Transaction Reconciliations:** Built-in capabilities to send and receive funds with customizable, backdated timestamps to match administrative records.
- **Lightweight, Responsive Experience:** An ultra-fast, mobile-first web app styled in rich violet and deep navy hues that functions flawlessly globally.

## Slide 5: The Product
A beautiful, responsive Single Page Application:
- **Instant Wallets:** Toggle USD/EUR/GBP accounts in real-time.
