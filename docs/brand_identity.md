# Meridian Trust Bank - Brand Identity & Positioning

## 1. Brand Positioning
Meridian Trust Bank is positioned as a **premier, lightweight offshore digital bank** engineered for high-growth startups, international businesses, and high-net-worth individuals (HNWIs). 
Unlike traditional brick-and-mortar offshore institutions that are slow, paper-heavy, and opaque, Meridian Trust combines the structural security and privacy of offshore jurisdictions with the instant, hyper-responsive user experience of modern fintech.

### Core Pillars
- **Frictionless Global Mobility:** Multi-currency accounts (USD, EUR, GBP) accessible in seconds, enabling seamless global commerce.
- **Privacy & Security:** Best-in-class multi-factor authentication, cryptographic security, and strict regulatory compliance.
- **Sophisticated Simplicity:** A user experience that feels premium, dark-themed, and lightweight—optimized to load instantly even under poor network conditions.
- **Operational Autonomy:** Powerful self-service features, including backdated transaction ledgers for historical reconciliation (crucial for offshore audits).

---

## 2. Logo Concepts

### Concept: The Meridian Sphere
- **Visual Description:** A minimalist, geometric wireframe sphere composed of intersecting vertical and horizontal meridians. A solid vault bar runs horizontally through the center, forming a stylized "M" and "T" monogram.
- **Symbolism:** The meridians represent global reach and borderless movement of wealth. The vault bar represents security, integrity, and trust.
- **Usage:** Ideal as an app icon, browser favicon, and physical debit card chip overlay.

## 3. Brand Guidelines & Visual Assets

### Color Palette
We employ a sophisticated **Dark Purple & Deep Sapphire** theme, contrasted with a premium **Meridian Teal** accent for high readability and a high-end fintech aesthetic.

| Role | Color Name | Hex Code | Visual Swatch / RGB |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Canvas** | Deep Space Navy | `#090D1A` | RGB(9, 13, 26) |
| **Surface Element**| Obsidian Purple | `#13112E` | RGB(19, 17, 46) |
| **Accent Primary** | Meridian Teal | `#06B6D4` | RGB(6, 182, 212) |
| **Accent Active**  | Electric Purple | `#A855F7` | RGB(168, 85, 247) |
| **Text Primary**   | Pure Platinum | `#F8FAFC` | RGB(248, 250, 252) |
| **Text Secondary** | Muted Slate | `#94A3B8` | RGB(148, 163, 184) |
| **Positive State** | Emerald Green | `#10B981` | RGB(16, 185, 129) |
| **Danger State**   | Crimson Red | `#EF4444` | RGB(239, 68, 68) |

### Typography
- **Headings (H1 - H4):** `Outfit` (sans-serif) — Rounded, geometric, and clean. Gives a premium digital-native banking feel.
- **Body Text & Numbers:** `Inter` (sans-serif) — Neutral, highly readable at small scale, excellent tabular numbers alignment.

### Design Elements
- **Glassmorphism:** Light transparent overlays (`rgba(19, 17, 46, 0.45)`) combined with a blur effect (`backdrop-filter: blur(16px)`).
- **Subtle Gradients:** Soft transitions between Dark Navy and Obsidian Purple to prevent visual fatigue.
- **Borders:** Thin, semi-transparent borders (`1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)`) to structure sections without heavy divisions.
